Welcome to the Transportation System

Road transportation involves the movement of goods and people using vehicles on roads, providing flexibility and accessibility.

Water transportation relies on ships and boats to move goods and passengers across bodies of water.

Air transportation enables fast travel over long distances using aircraft.

A Transportation Management System (TMS) is a software platform designed to plan, execute, and optimize the movement of goods and services. It helps businesses streamline their transportation operations by automating tasks such as route planning, carrier selection, load optimization, and real-time tracking.
